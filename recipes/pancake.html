Pancake

Description

One of the most simplest breakfast to make. Just the thing to get you going for the day. This recipe takes under 20 minute to serve.

Add honey or syrup for more deliciousness :)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a hole and add the wet ingredients in. Mix well.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
  6. Enjoy the sweet pancakies!!
