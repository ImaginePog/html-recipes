Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round base of wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

This recipe provides the ingredients needed and steps to make a delicious pizza! Credits to Jamie Oliver for this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • Strong white bread flour
  • Semolina flour or strong white bread flour
  • Fine sea salt
  • Dried yeast
  • Golden caster sugar
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Add sea salt to the flour.
  2. Add yeast and sugar to the mixture with water and leave for a few minutes.
  3. Knead the dough until soft.
  4. Let the dough sit for around 45 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a pan, add the garlic and basil then cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes and salt + pepper.
  7. Make a circular base.
  8. Put sauce on the base.
  9. Cook the pizza in an oven.
  10. Enjoy the food!!
