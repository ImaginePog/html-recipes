Chef John's Pasta Primavera

Description

Pasta primavera is quite a straightforward recipe; spaghetti or fettuccine tossed with an array of fresh spring vegetables.

Credits to Chef John for this delicous recipe.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Chicken broth
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Zucchini
  • Peas
  • Asparagus
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water with salt and add the greens.
  2. Discard basil stems.
  3. Blend basil leaves and chicken broth with garlic.
  4. Stir fettuccine into the same pot of boiling water, bring back to a boil.
  5. Heat oil in a pan, add the garlic and basil then cook for a few minutes.
  6. Cook leek and onion in hot oil.
  7. Put everything together and cook until aspagarus is soft.
  8. Pour zucchini mixture over the pasta and spread cheese on top.
  9. Enjoy the food!!
